= Welcome to RHDP MaaS
:navtitle: Welcome

RHDP Model-as-a-Service (MaaS) gives you access to production AI models running on OpenShift AI — through a single OpenAI-compatible API.
Your virtual key is scoped to the models you selected at order time.
No infrastructure to manage, no separate accounts.

== What you will learn

In this workshop you will:

* Call AI models with curl and the OpenAI Python SDK
* Use text embeddings for semantic search and RAG pipelines
* Count tokens before sending requests to estimate cost and context usage
* Convert PDFs and documents to Markdown with Docling

== Who this is for

This workshop is designed for developers and platform engineers who want to use AI models in demos, workshops, and agentic workflows running on RHDP infrastructure.

== Your environment

Your lab includes:

* An OpenShift SNO cluster with a terminal available in the *Terminal* tab
* A LiteMaaS virtual key scoped to your selected models — visible in the *Setup & Credentials* section
* The OpenShift Console embedded in the *OCP Console* tab
